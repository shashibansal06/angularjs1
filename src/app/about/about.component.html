The story of Lychees

What is Lychees.io

We are the worlds first gamified UX and CX optimisation platform focusing our efforts on continuous improvement after products and websites go live. This allows our customers to deploy products fast and at lower cost and shift to meet the needs of consumers.

Our Mission

Lychees is founded on the belief that an active relationship between consumers and service providers through feedback can produce better products and results for a business.

Our mission is to change the way the general population interacts with online and offline products. Dissatisfaction with product features is currently a dead end for consumers with very little recourse past moving onto an alternative product. Providing feedback usually requires and investment of time and effort that gives zero return for the consumer. Our aim is the change the sentiment of dissatisfaction into a positive experience and opportunity.

By rewarding feedback with a fair system we can change attitudes and create a loop between the consumer and product development team to allow for improvements against consumer needs.

About us

We are an experienced team of marketing, design and development professionals that are seeking to change the way product managers develop their future roadmap for aligning with the consumers needs.

Our platform aims to create a better feedback loop between the end user and the product or service by empowering the end user with the responsibility for improving the product and rewarding them for taking up the challenge of providing quality feedback.
